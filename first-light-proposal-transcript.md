## Slide 2 — Marketing Funnel

Like the first light of a new day, every customer journey begins with discovery. Strategic marketing nurtures that journey into trust, action and lasting loyalty.

**Marketing Funnel** (5 stages, top to bottom): Attract → Engage → Convert → Retain → Advocate

- **ATTRACT: Helping people discover your business.**
  This stage is about increasing visibility and putting your brand in front of the right audience.

- **ENGAGE: Building trust and creating connection.**
  Once people know you exist, they need a reason to choose you. This stage nurtures relationships through valuable, consistent marketing.

- **RETAIN: Keeping customers engaged.**
  Great marketing doesn't stop after the first sale. This stage focuses on building loyalty and encouraging repeat business.

- **CONVERT: Turning interest into action.**
  This stage encourages customers to take the next step, whether that's making a purchase, booking a service or getting in touch.

- **ADVOCATE: Turning customers into brand ambassadors.**
  Satisfied customers become your biggest supporters by leaving reviews, sharing their experiences and recommending your business.

With experience across every stage of the customer journey, I create marketing that doesn't just look good, it delivers results.

*(Note: on the slide, RETAIN and CONVERT appear in that visual order in the right-hand column even though the funnel graphic on the left lists them as Attract, Engage, Convert, Retain, Advocate — use the logical order: Attract, Engage, Convert, Retain, Advocate.)*
